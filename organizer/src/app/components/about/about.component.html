M I S S I O N




Thousands of individuals across the United States depend on the generosity of an organ donor to stay alive.


Our app is designed to make the connection between donors and recipients via healthcare facilities to hopefully give more individuals the chance at a longer and healthier life.




OUR PROCESS


HOW WE MAKE MATCHES




BONE MARROW

Bone Marrow recipients and donors are matched based on HLA (Human Leukocyte Antigen) proteins and tissue. All humans share the major and minor classes of HLA proteins. For bone marrow transplant matching, doctors identify how close the variations of these proteins are between donors and recipients.

HEART

In general, matches for heart transplants are made based on proximity of recipients to the hospital. Once removed from the body, hearts can be preserved for only about 4 to 6 hours. Our system currently matches heart transplant recipients with donors based on HLA compatability.

KIDNEY

Kidney matches are made based on age of the recipient, blood type, HLA compatiblity, and other factors. ORGANIZR makes kidney matches based on HLA compatibility.
